Bio
======

Hey there! 👋 I'm **Fan Li**, a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Central Florida (UCF).

I spend most of my time thinking about how to make memory systems **faster, smarter, and more reliable** — from squeezing extra bandwidth out of DDR5, to protecting data inside emerging accelerators. My work sits at the intersection of **computer architecture** and **reliable computing**, and I'm always excited when a clever hardware trick can solve a real-world problem.
